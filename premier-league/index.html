Idea we're exploring

Head-to-Head leagues

Alongside the Classic table, we're looking at a Head-to-Head format: each gameweek you'd be drawn against one other member, and whoever posts the higher gameweek score wins the match — 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw — with the table ranked like a real league. Every week becomes a personal duel: forget topping the whole table, you'd just need to beat your opponent.

Like the sound of it? Register your interest below and tell us — it'll help shape what we build.

Another idea

Last Man Standing

Pick one Premier League team to win each gameweek — survive and you're through, but a draw or a defeat and you're out, and you can't reuse a team. We're looking at running it in short rounds alongside the main game, so when one ends another kicks off and you can jump back in.

Fancy it too? Tell us when you register your interest.
